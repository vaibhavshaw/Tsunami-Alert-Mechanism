Tsunami Cause Code:
Valid values: 0 to 11
The source of the tsunami:

0 = Unknown
1 = Earthquake
2 = Questionable Earthquake
3 = Earthquake and Landslide
4 = Volcano and Earthquake
5 = Volcano, Earthquake, and Landslide
6 = Volcano
7 = Volcano and Landslide
8 = Landslide
9 = Meteorological
10 = Explosion
11 = Astronomical Tide
